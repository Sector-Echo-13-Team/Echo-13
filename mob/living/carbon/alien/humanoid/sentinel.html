Alien Sentinel
/mob/living/carbon/alien/humanoid/sentinel

A slightly more durable but less mobile xeno caste. Utilizes neurotoxin to bring down prey, and has a unique cloaking ability.

A caste of alien which prefers to act more at a range and with stealth compared to the hunter. While it is slower than the average human, its neurotoxin spit allows to to stun a target from afar so it can close the distance and capture prey. To assist with this, the sentinel also has the unique ability to become barely visible, allowing it to ambush targets and evade pursuers, especially in the dark or when standing on resin.
