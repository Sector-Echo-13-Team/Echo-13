Alien Queen
/mob/living/carbon/alien/humanoid/royal/queen

The most important alien caste, the queen is the only reproductive member of the set.

A subtype of alien which acts as the sole reproductive member of the alien castes. The queen boasts the most health and damage of all the xeno types, but is also the slowest. She can lay eggs which will create facehuggers, promote other xenos to praetorians, and also call_shuttle the emergency shuttle after 15 minutes of being alive.
