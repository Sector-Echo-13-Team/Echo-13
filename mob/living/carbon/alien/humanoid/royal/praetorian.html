Alien Praetorian
/mob/living/carbon/alien/humanoid/royal/praetorian

A larget, tankier alien who wields an impressive amount of health at the cost of speed and ventcrawling.

A subtype of alien which acts as a tanky bruiser. Like the queen, it can use tail whip to stun enemies, and has access to neurotoxin and acid, along with being able to create structures via resin spinner. It is also the only caste which can evolve into alien, albeit only if one currently does not exist and the limit for queens in a round hasn't already been met.
